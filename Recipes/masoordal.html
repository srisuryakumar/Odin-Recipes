Masoor Dal

Masoor Dal also known as Red Lentil Curry, is a popular and nutritious Indian dish made with red lentils cooked in a flavorful blend of spices. It is a comforting and satisfying dish that is commonly served with rice or roti.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils (masoor dal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the red lentils thoroughly and soak them in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and mushy.
  6. Drain the soaked lentils and add them to the pan. Stir well.
  7. Add water (approximately 2 cups) and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 20 minutes or until the lentils are cooked and tender.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
