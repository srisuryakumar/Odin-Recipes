Palak Tofu

Palak Tofu, also known as Spinach Tofu Curry, is a flavorful and nutritious Indian dish that combines fresh spinach leaves with tofu, creating a wholesome and satisfying vegetarian or vegan option.

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams tofu, cubed
  • 2 cups spinach leaves
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the spinach leaves in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Drain and transfer them to a blender. Blend to a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for a minute.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add the spinach puree and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Gently add the tofu cubes and simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.
