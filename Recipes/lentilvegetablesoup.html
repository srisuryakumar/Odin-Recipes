Lentil and Vegetable Soup

Lentil and Vegetable Soup is a hearty and nutritious dish that combines the goodness of lentils with a variety of vegetables, creating a comforting and flavorful soup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed lentils (red lentils, yellow lentils, green lentils), soaked
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, paprika, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and mushy.
  5. Add the soaked lentils and mix well.
  6. Add water (approximately 4 cups) and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes or until the lentils are cooked and the vegetables are tender.
  7. Adjust the seasoning if needed. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot.
