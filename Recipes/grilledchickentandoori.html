Grilled Tandoori Chicken Skewers

Tandoori Grilled Chicken Skewers are a delicious and flavorful Indian dish that features marinated chicken pieces skewered and grilled to perfection. It is a popular dish known for its smoky and charred flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Onion rings and lemon wedges for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, tandoori masala, ginger paste, garlic paste, red chili powder, salt, and oil to make a marinade.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat the grill or grill pan.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto skewers.
  5. Grill the chicken skewers on medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and have a charred appearance.
  6. Garnish with onion rings and lemon wedges. Serve hot with mint chutney.
